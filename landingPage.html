About BankNest

BankNest is your trusted digital banking platform designed to make financial transactions fast, secure, and convenient. Whether you're sending money to loved ones, depositing funds, or managing your account, BankNest gives you full control over your finances from anywhere.

Our mission is to bring banking closer to everyone by offering seamless digital services, smart tools, and a simple user interface. With BankNest, you can enjoy transparency, reliability, and 24/7 access to your account and transaction history.

Key Features

User Registration & Multi-Step Signup

Complete registration across multiple pages capturing personal details, identity verification, and account setup.

JWT-Based Authentication

Secure login with token-based authentication ensuring safe access.

Account Management

View, create, and manage your bank accounts with ease.

Transactions

Deposit, transfer, and review transaction history with PIN verification.

Loan Management

Apply for loans, make repayments, and manage penalties seamlessly.

Real-time Complaint Chat

Chat with support officers directly via SignalR-enabled live chat.

File Upload

Upload profile images securely during registration.

Validation & Security

Robust frontend & backend validation including regex checks, age verification, PIN & password strength, and 2FA support.

Instant Transfers

Send money to any account instantly with real-time updates.

Bank Selection
