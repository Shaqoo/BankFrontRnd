Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I check my account balance?
    Go to the "Accounts" tab and click on your account to view the balance.
  • How can I apply for a loan?
    Navigate to "Loans" and click "Apply for a Loan".
  • I Want To Update My Profile. What do I do?
    Click "Profile" on the Sidebar and follow the instructions.
